who we are...

Discover a universe of cycling marvels under our roof. Our showroom is a universe of spinning wheels, intricate designs, and cutting-edge materials. Whether you're a casual rider or a hardcore cyclist, our collection caters to your aspirations, inviting you to join the global cycling community.


Beyond the bikes themselves, CycloSphere is a gateway to a global cycling community. We don't just sell bikes; we foster connections, camaraderie, and shared experiences. Our showroom is a launchpad for friendships formed on group rides, a hub for discovering local cycling events, and a source of inspiration for those seeking to become part of a worldwide movement. By choosing a bike from CycloSphere, you're not just acquiring a mode of transport; you're becoming a part of something much larger.
